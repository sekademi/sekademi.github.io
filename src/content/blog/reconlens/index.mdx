In the fast-paced world of cybersecurity, information is the most valuable currency. **ReconLens** is designed to give security researchers, bug bounty hunters, and penetration testers a powerful, passive lens into the modern web.

ReconLens has been expanded to meet the needs of today's security professionals. It operates silently in your browser, analyzing every page you visit to harvest intelligence without sending a single extra request.

## 🚀 Key Features

ReconLens is packed with features designed for speed, accuracy, and ease of use:

- **Passive Extraction**: Analyzes page source, inline scripts, and referenced JS files in real-time.
- **Deep Normalization**: Automatically cleans and deduplicates harvested data, ensuring you get high-quality results.
- **13 Detection Categories**: From IPs and domains to JWTs and sensitive paths, ReconLens covers the most critical data points.
- **700+ Secret Patterns**: Powered by a robust regex engine to detect API keys, tokens, and credentials.
- **AI-Ready Export**: One-click "Copy for AI" functionality that formats results for advanced analysis.

## 🧠 Smart Intelligence

What sets ReconLens apart is its ability to distinguish between different types of data intelligently. It can resolve relative paths to correct target URLs, perform quick lookups on Shodan or VirusTotal, and even forward findings to your own webhook for centralized tracking.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Performance

Security tools shouldn't slow you down or compromise your privacy. ReconLens performs all analysis locally in your browser. No data leaves your machine unless you explicitly configure a webhook. With its optimized regex matching and auto-timeout features, it remains lightweight even on the most script-heavy sites.

## Conclusion

ReconLens isn't just an extension; it's a silent partner in your reconnaissance workflow. By automating the extraction of hidden intelligence, it allows you to focus on what matters most: finding vulnerabilities and securing the web.
